About Us

Performing Arts

The Art Of Movement

Success is often inspired by creativity. And, creativity is synonymous with performing arts. Creativity, performing arts and a little bit of imagination have proven to be the foundation stones for an alluring personality displaying confidence, élan and grace in equal measures.

We at ‘Dancepiration’ will not only shape your artistic performance capabilities but also groom all aspects of your personality. We will challenge you physically, spiritually and intellectually so that you discover the magic of your true creative self.

SNEH GUPTA, our extremely talented, experienced and skilled founder, takes up the challenge to make DANCE an ASPIRATION for you; to ensure that you continuously and comprehensively nurture your creative side. At the same time, she guarantees a learning environment that is not only encouraging and inspiring but also great fun; a perfect blend of physical stimulation and mental relaxation.

So, come meet us at ‘Dancepiration’. We will ensure that your dreams and destiny unite to become one as you soar higher and higher in the world of performing arts.
