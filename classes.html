Dance

Nobody cares if you can't dance well. Just get up and dance
We expose our students to a very wide range of commercial dance styles. We focus on following core dances: Contemporary, Hip-Hop, Jazz, Ballet, Salsa, Belly Dance, Bollywood, Urban, Dancehall, Dancehouse, B-Boying, Classical, Folk.

Fitness

Life begins at the end of your comfort zone
Dancepiration Fitness program is the solution for your busy schedule. Our trainer trains you at your location when it is most convenient for you. We work on shaping your body with unlimited dance and fun. We majorly focus on Zumba as a person can burn up to 600 calories just in one Zumba class, depending on the energy put forth.

Gymnastics

You know you're a gymnast when you're on your hands as much as your feet
We focus on body flexibility and muscle toning as a part of our Gymnastics program. Gymnastics has been a popular excercise to strengthen body movements and our center of gravity. At Dancepiration Gymnastics program, our goal is to provide fun, skill based classes to motivate kids for a lifetime of fitness. We offer Gymnastics for kids beginning at 10 months.

Events

A jolly dance can make all events pleasant
Each of our events is different and each group is unique so it is our job to incorporate those unique elements so that you feel at ease, and are able to just enjoy the time with your guests. We love to brainstorm, create, and bring into reality your dream event within your budget. We specially focus on Birthday party events, Marriage events, Corporate events, Annual events and School field trips. Our Birthday parties will include unlimited dance events, 45 minutes game session, basic decoration and unique themes. Our Marriage events will include Sangeet and Dance choreography and we also provide dancers for marraige events.

Martial Arts

The more you sweat in training, the less you bleed in combat
If you are seeking martial arts training for recreational and fitness purposes; or for self-defence and personal protection skills; or even as a therapy for yourself or your child, we believe training at The Dancepiration Martial Arts program will Make a Positive Difference in Your Life. We majorly focus on Taekwondo, which is characterized by its emphasis on head-height kicks, jumping and spinning kicks, and fast kicking techniques.

Theatre

Acting is behaving truthfully under imaginary circumstances
In the Dancepiration's Theatre program, the student will be guided through a journey of self-exploration and uncover acting techniques that will allow them master the craft of Film and Television performing. In each block, you will be prepared for the challenges of the next course ahead as every module of work intensifies and builds upon the latter. This is an inspiring and life-changing acting course, it allows students to develop skills necessary to carve out a career in acting by experiencing the entire scope of performing, from live performance in the theatre to acting in an advanced film project.
